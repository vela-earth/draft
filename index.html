Who we are

We align global innovation with local reality.

A sail does not create force — it captures and directs it. VELA is the art of moving forward by harnessing the forces around you: people, ecosystems, capital, and context.

We sit at the intersection of climate, agriculture, and commerce, working with European solution providers who need a trusted, well-networked local partner to navigate complexity and turn ambition into real projects in Southern Africa.

Unlocking Dutch & EU co-financing for your South Africa entry

One of VELA's most concrete value propositions is helping clients access Dutch government instruments — and serving as the in-country partner that makes these instruments accessible.

RVO · NL

DHI — Demo, Feasibility & Investment Prep

Co-finance for feasibility studies and demo projects in South Africa. VELA serves as the required in-country local partner. Open until 31 Dec 2026.

Up to €200k (demo) / €100k (feasibility)

RVO · NL

Impact Clusters

NL consortia + local partner introducing proven technology in AgriTech, water or climate. VELA as your local anchor partner. Open until 1 Sept 2026.

Substantial per cluster

RVO · NL

PIB — Partners for International Business

Public-private programme for 5+ Dutch companies entering SA together. SA precedent exists (Waste & Circular Economy cluster). VELA facilitates the cluster.

~€350k per programme

Invest International · NL

DGGF / DTIF — Tailored Finance

Loans, guarantees and investment for SMEs expanding to South Africa. Entry via a free quick scan through Invest International. ZA is a DGGF country.

Tailored financing on request
